A list of things that I enjoy

By: AJ Engebretson

In no particular order

1) Walker and Wylde

6000 - 7000 monthly listeners

Walker and Wylde were two homeless guys living in one van as they traveled from coast to coast in Canada. they made Indie folk/ blue grass music, busking for money whenever they could. they dropped their first episode on January 1st, 2021 labeled "There Are Gods in Little Men." They periodically dropped new songs, until Febuary 2nd 2022, when they dropped their first album "Secondhand Existential Crisis."
